The Process

Your Path to
Retirement Clarity

We keep it simple. Here's exactly what happens when you work with John.

  1. 01

    Full Pension Review

    We'll gather all your pension details and create a single, clear picture of what you have, how much it's costing you, and how it's performing.

  2. 02

    Consolidation Assessment

    Is consolidating right for you? We'll explore whether combining pensions makes sense — looking at charges, flexibility, and your specific circumstances.

  3. 03

    Retirement Income Projection

    Working backwards from your retirement dreams, we'll show you how much income your pensions can generate and whether it's enough.

  4. 04

    Action & Ongoing Support

    We'll handle the paperwork and coordinate with your pension providers. Then we'll stay in touch, reviewing your plan annually as circumstances change.
